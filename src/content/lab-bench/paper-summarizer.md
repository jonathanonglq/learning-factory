---
title: Research Summarizer
description: A reading assistant that separates paper claims, evidence, assumptions, and follow-up experiments.
date: 2026-09-18
---

The Research Summarizer is meant to slow down paper reading rather than speed it up. Instead of producing a generic abstract, it separates what the paper claims, what evidence supports those claims, what assumptions are doing hidden work, and what experiments would test the idea further.

This structure makes the output less polished but more useful. A paper summary should not only say what happened. It should make it easier to decide whether the result is robust, relevant, and worth implementing.

The next step is to evaluate summaries against hand-written notes from papers I know well. If the assistant cannot preserve the parts I personally care about, it is only compressing text rather than supporting understanding.
